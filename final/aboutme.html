What do you need to know?

Hi!! My name is Emerson. Long story short, I was born in Maryland in 1999 and I lived there until age 13, when I moved to Highlands Ranch, Colorado. I then moved to Boulder to attend the University of Colorado, Boulder where I am currently majoring in Creative Technology and Design and minoring in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology. I am also the co-president of the Herd Leadership Council, work at the Environmental Center, and play a lot of intramural sports. Outside of school and work, I enjoy spending time with my family and friends and doing any outdoor activity, especially hiking and snowboarding. I love living in Colorado and am so happy to be a part of the CTD program here.
